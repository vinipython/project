## Reset Password

Follow these steps to reset your password.

1. Visit the Glean dashboard and click log in.
2. Click **Forgot Password**.
3. Enter your email address and click **Submit**.
    ![password reset image](./images/land.png)
4. Open your email inbox and click the reset link.
5. The Glean password reset page opens. Enter the new password.
    > **Note**: Ensure you meet the password requirements
    > - Must be of 8-12 characters.
    > - At least two numbers and one special character.  
6. Re-enter the password to confirm it.

This resets your password. Log in to your account with the new password to continue.
